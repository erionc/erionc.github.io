Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Formal and Applied linguistics, Charles University in Prague, working on the ELITR (European Live Translator) project. My research interests include Machine/Deep Learning for Natural Language Processing and Text Mining, especially Text Summarization of meeting dialogues.

I was previously a PhD student in the SoftEng Group at Politecnico di Torino university in Italy where I finished my thesis on Text-based Sentiment Analysis and Music Emotion Recogniton. I also worked in a collaborative project about On-Car Music Recommender Systems within JOL MobiLab of TIM.
