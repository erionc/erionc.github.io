Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute of Formal and Applied linguistics, Charles University in Prague, working on the ELITR (European Live Translator) project. My main research focus is machine learning and deep learning for natural language processing, especially text summarization of dialogues and meeting discussions (minuting) and Natural Language Generation (NLG) evaluation.

I was previously a PhD student in the SoftEng Group at Politecnico di Torino university in Italy where I finished my thesis on Text-based Sentiment Analysis and Music Emotion Recogniton. I also worked in a collaborative project about On-Car Music Recommender Systems within JOL MobiLab of TIM.
